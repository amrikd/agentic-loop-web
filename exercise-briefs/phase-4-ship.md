# Phase 4 — SHIP
### 35 minutes | Polish, document, prepare to present

---

## Your Goal

Make your app competition-ready. Polish the UI, write documentation, and prepare for the group showcase. This is where the **70% output quality score** gets won.

## What to Do

1. **Switch to the Shipper agent** in VS Code (or use the handoff from Reviewer)
2. **Split your time:**

### Polish the UI (15 min)

Pick 3–4 high-impact improvements:

**Quick wins (5 min each):**
- Mood button animation on selection (scale, bounce, glow)
- Smooth color transitions between states
- Character count that turns red near 280
- Success animation after submit (checkmark, confetti, fade-in)

**Medium effort (10 min each):**
- Chart bars animate on load (grow from zero)
- Staggered fade-in for comment list
- Loading skeletons instead of spinners
- Relative timestamps ("2 hours ago" not ISO strings)

**Stretch goals:**
- Keyboard shortcuts (1–5 to select mood)
- Auto-refresh dashboard every 30 seconds
- Dark/light mode toggle
- Haptic feedback (mobile)

### Write Documentation (10 min)

Ask the agent to generate:
- **PR description** — what you built, key decisions, testing approach
- **README updates** — how to run, what it does

### Prepare for Showcase (10 min)

Your group will pick one app to present. Be ready to:
1. **Demo it** — submit a mood, show the dashboard updating (30 sec)
2. **Explain where AI helped** — what worked well (30 sec)
3. **Explain where AI failed** — what you had to fix or override (30 sec)
4. **Share what changes** for your next sprint (30 sec)

Think about:
- What's the most impressive thing your app does?
- What did AI generate that surprised you?
- What did you catch that AI missed?

## If You're Stuck

**Not sure what to polish?**
> "Look at my app and suggest the 3 highest-impact visual improvements I can make in 15 minutes."

**Need a PR description?**
> "Write a PR description for everything I built. Include architecture decisions, testing approach, and what I'd do next."

## Don't

- ❌ Start new features — polish what you have
- ❌ Forget documentation — it's part of the 30% rigor score
- ❌ Wait until the last minute to think about your showcase

## Output

✅ Polished, competition-ready app
✅ PR description in `PR_DESCRIPTION.md`
✅ Ready to present to your group
